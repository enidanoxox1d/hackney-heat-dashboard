How the Score Works

Negative factors create a vulnerability burden. Positive adaptation actions reduce that burden.

Final Raw ScoreTotal Positive Score + Total Negative Score
Final Vulnerability Score100 - (((Final Raw Score + 1) / 2) × 100)

A higher vulnerability score means the school is more exposed or less prepared. This keeps the map intuitive: higher score = higher risk.
